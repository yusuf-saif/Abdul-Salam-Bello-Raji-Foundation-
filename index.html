Vision & Mission

Healthier, empowered, self-sustaining communities.

Our vision is a world where every individual has access to basic needs, healthcare, and educational opportunities. We pursue it by serving humanity with care and mercy.

01

Accessible preventive healthcare

We provide screenings, basic preventive services and health education through community outreach events for underserved populations.

02

Educational opportunity for the underprivileged

From learning materials to scholarships and Quranic education support, we help children and young adults stay in school and move forward.

03

Local partnerships, lasting impact

We collaborate with local organizations, healthcare professionals and volunteers — and we facilitate access to essential provisions for families facing financial challenges.

From Our Founder

"Service to humanity is service to the Most Merciful."

This foundation was born from a simple conviction: that no one — no child, no elder, no struggling family — should be left without dignity, healthcare, or the chance to learn. We carry that conviction across two homes, Atlanta and Ibadan, and into every community we are invited to serve.

We are not a large organization. We are a careful one. Every screening, every scholarship, every meal we deliver is touched by hands that know the weight of what is being given and received.

If our work moves you, walk with us. There is room for you here.

A. Bello Raji
AbdulSalam Bello Raji Founder · AbdulSalam Bello Raji Foundation

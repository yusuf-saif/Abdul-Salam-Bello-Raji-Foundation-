Programs & Initiatives

Compassionate care, delivered with dignity.

At the AbdulSalam Bello Raji Foundation, we are committed to improving the well-being of vulnerable individuals and families through community support, healthcare outreach, and humanitarian assistance. Our initiatives focus on addressing basic needs, promoting health, and empowering communities.

What We Do

Six programs working as one.

Support a program →
01

Community Health Support

We organize community-based health initiatives that help underserved populations access basic health education, screenings, and preventive care support. These programs aim to promote healthier communities and raise awareness about common health conditions.

  • Free blood pressure, blood sugar and basic vitals screenings
  • One-on-one health education and lifestyle counseling
  • Referrals to partner clinics for follow-up care
  • Maternal and child health awareness sessions
Health
02

Support for the Elderly

The foundation provides assistance to elderly individuals who may lack access to essential care and support. Through our outreach programs, we aim to improve their well-being and ensure they receive dignity, attention, and compassionate care.

  • Monthly food and provisions deliveries
  • Companion visits and social engagement programs
  • Coordination with healthcare providers for routine checkups
  • Support during religious and community festivals
Elderly
03

Education Support

We support educational initiatives that help children from disadvantaged backgrounds continue their learning journey. This may include educational materials, scholarships, and learning support programs.

  • School materials, uniforms and stationery distributions
  • Scholarship grants for primary and secondary students
  • After-school learning support and tutoring
  • Back-to-school awareness and enrollment drives
Education
04

Quranic Scholarship Initiative

Faith-rooted scholarships supporting students of Quranic education. We honor tradition while opening doors to broader learning, mentorship, and opportunity for promising students from low-income families.

  • Tuition support for Quranic memorization students
  • Boarding, meals and learning materials
  • Mentorship from established teachers and scholars
  • Bridge programs into formal academic education
Faith
05

Food & Nutrition Outreach

We facilitate access to essential provisions for individuals and families facing financial challenges. Community nutrition outreach delivered with care — never charity-as-spectacle.

  • Monthly food parcels for vulnerable families
  • Special distributions during Ramadan and Eid
  • Nutrition education for mothers and caregivers
  • Partnerships with local food banks and farmers
Food
06

Volunteer & Community Engagement

Volunteers are the heart of the foundation. We welcome students, healthcare professionals, community members, and skilled volunteers in IT, media and administration — anyone passionate about helping others.

  • Health support: outreach events, patient registration
  • Community outreach: nutrition programs, food drives
  • Education & scholarship coordination
  • Administrative & digital support: media, data, events
